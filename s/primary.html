PRE-PRIMARY AND PRIMARY

Welcome to the Pre-Primary and Primary School, where we nurture the development of each child emotionally, academically, physically, socially and artistically every day. During these formative years, it is essential that children build a strong foundation of basic skills, a deep sense of self and an ability to contribute to a group while capitalizing on their innate curiosity about the world around them. Within a challenging curriculum, children are encouraged to take risks and allowed to make mistakes in a supportive environment, under the care of committed teachers.
