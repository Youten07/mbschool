Why Mahabouddha

"A SPIRIT THAT NEVER DIES."

As someone once rightly quoted 'if you give a man a fish to eat, he will eat for a day. But, if you teach a man how to fish, he will eat everyday.' Mahabouddha provides an optimised learning enviroment where students learn all the ife snd soft skills that they need. Our highly exprienced teachers make sure that no child getsleft behind.

Laxman Thapa(Principal)

The learning and development of our students is not confined to the classrooms but is extended to the external enviroment, which allows students to explore their surroundings and exercise their imagination. We believe students should be motivated to be lifelong learners, problem slovers, critical thinkers, and above all, productive members of and ever-changing global society.

Miraj Rana (Math Teacher)

Five years of a child's education are vital for developing their critical thinking and problem sloving skills and as such, we have created an enviroment in which our young students feel empowerd to ask question, safe to meet challenges ahead on and accompained on the journey towards reaching their full potentail.
